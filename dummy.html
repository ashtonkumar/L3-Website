Scope

We are a boutique consultancy firm providing engineering design services for your building projects. Our services will assist you in fulfilling the requirements of a resource and building consent which requires specialist input from either a Civil, Structural, or Geotechnical engineering professional. Our passion is to offer buildable solutions through innovative engineering design.

Our core values are honesty, integrity, competence, and ownership. Our values allow our team to provide our clients with professional engineering designs with peace of mind that your project can ease from construction through to completion.

Our team is focused on meeting the needs of your budget while delivering fit-for-purpose engineering design solutions that consider saving material costs during construction and minimizing or eliminating maintenance costs of your completed building project...
